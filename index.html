Kingdom of Light

Morocco

History & Traditions

A land of ancient dynasties and Berber resilience. Morocco is a tapestry of Roman ruins, Islamic scholarship, and French artistry. To visit is to step into a living history that spans from the High Atlas to the Atlantic coast.

Culinary Arts

Moroccan cuisine is a sensory explosion. Saffron, ginger, and turmeric define the Tagines, while Mint Tea acts as the heartbeat of social hospitality. Note: Always greet and eat with your right hand.

Weather & Attire

Jan 17–24 brings the "Crystal Winter." Expect bright, sunny days (18°C) and sharp, cold nights (5°C). We recommend high-quality wool layers and comfortable footwear for the cobblestones of Fes.
