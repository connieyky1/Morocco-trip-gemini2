Introduction

Kingdom of Light

Overview

Morocco is a sensory tapestry where ancient history meets a vibrant contemporary pulse. From the red walls of Marrakesh to the labyrinthine alleys of Fes, you will encounter a culture defined by hospitality and artisan mastery.

The Journey (Jan 17-24)

Weather: Expect mild days (18°C) and crisp evenings (6°C). We recommend layering with light linens for the day and a tailored wool coat for Fes and the Desert evenings.

Etiquette

Dress modestly, particularly in the Medina. Always ask before photographing local artisans. A small tip (10-20 Dirhams) is appreciated for services.
